## Introduction

Welcome to Geometry Game! This is a simple yet engaging game where you'll practice your geometry skills by guessing point coordinates and calculating squares of rectangles. Perfect for students, educators, or anyone looking to improve their understanding of basic geometric concepts.

The primary workflow involves running the game, making guesses, and seeing if you're correct. The main advantages include:

- **Interactive Learning**: Engage with geometry in a fun and interactive way.
- **Immediate Feedback**: Get instant feedback on your answers.
- **Educational Value**: Enhance understanding of basic geometric concepts.

## Features

### Guess Point Coordinates
- **What it does**: Players guess the coordinates of a randomly placed point within a grid.
- **Why it exists**: To practice and improve spatial awareness and coordinate geometry skills.
- **Why it is useful**: Helps in developing an intuitive understanding of how to locate points on a plane.

### Calculate Squares of Rectangles
- **What it does**: Players calculate the area of a rectangle given its length and width.
- **Why it exists**: To practice and improve arithmetic and geometric calculations.
- **Why it is useful**: Enhances problem-solving skills and reinforces basic mathematical concepts.

## How It Works

The game consists of two main modes:

1. **Guess Point Coordinates Mode**:
   - A point is randomly placed on a grid.
   - Players are prompted to guess the coordinates of the point.
   - The game provides feedback on whether the guess is correct or not.

2. **Calculate Squares of Rectangles Mode**:
   - Players are given the length and width of a rectangle.
   - They need to calculate the area of the rectangle.
   - The game checks if the calculated area is correct.
